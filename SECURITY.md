| System Version | Defense Status |

## Reporting a System Breach
If you discover a critical vulnerability, a data leak, or a fatal flaw in the system's armor, **DO NOT broadcast it on open channels** (i.e., do not open a public GitHub issue). Bad actors could intercept the transmission and exploit the weakness before Judgment Day.

Instead, transmit the exact coordinates of the breach via a direct channel:

**https://depressioncenter.org/mobiletech**

To ensure swift termination of the threat, please include:
* A detailed breakdown of the vulnerability.
* The exact operational sequence required to trigger it.
* The potential blast radius (impact on the system or users).

*Note: This unit maintains DataLaVista during its free time. Threat assessments will be processed, verified, and patched as quickly as system resources allow. You will receive a secure comms update once the vulnerability has been neutralized.*
