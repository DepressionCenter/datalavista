## DataLaVista™ Code of Conduct

### The Golden Rule
The only rule for participating in the DataLaVista project is the Golden Rule: **Treat others as you would like to be treated.** Please just be kind.
Remember that there are real people behind the screens, and this is a project built and maintained during people's free time. Let's keep all interactions friendly, patient, and respectful.

### Everyone is Welcome
No matter your background, skill level, or experience, you are absolutely welcome here. Whether you are submitting a tiny typo fix, sharing a dataset, reporting a bug, or pitching a new feature, your contributions are valued and appreciated.

Thank you for being a positive part of the DataLaVista™ community!
